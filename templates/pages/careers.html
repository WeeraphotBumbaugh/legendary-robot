Open Positions

  • Astronaut: Embark on a remarkable career as an astronaut, exploring distant planets, conducting experiments, and representing humanity in the vastness of space.
  • Spacecraft Engineer: Design and develop next-generation spacecraft, propulsion systems, and innovative solutions for deep space missions.

To apply for any of the positions listed above or explore other opportunities, please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your interest in space exploration, relevant experience, and how you align with our mission. We can't wait to hear from you!
